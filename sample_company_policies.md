# Sample Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy

Our company supports remote work arrangements for eligible employees. Here are the key guidelines:

### Eligibility
- Full-time employees with 6+ months of tenure
- Employees with satisfactory performance reviews
- Roles that can be performed remotely

### Requirements
- Dedicated workspace at home
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Available during core hours (9 AM - 3 PM EST)
- Participate in weekly team meetings

### Equipment
- Company laptop and monitor provided
- Home office stipend of $500 annually
- VPN access for secure connections

### Communication
- Daily check-ins with supervisor
- Use company Slack for team communication
- Email response within 4 hours during business hours

## IT Support

### Getting IT Support
- Submit tickets through our IT portal: support.company.com
- Call IT helpdesk: (555) 123-4567
- Available Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM EST

### Common Issues
- Password resets: Use self-service portal
- Software installation: Submit approval request first
- Hardware issues: Contact helpdesk immediately

### VPN Access
- Download VPN client from IT portal
- Use company credentials to connect
- Required for accessing internal systems remotely

## Employee Benefits

### Health Insurance
- Company covers 80% of premium costs
- Multiple plan options available
- Coverage begins on first day of employment

### Vacation Policy
- 15 days PTO for new employees
- 20 days after 3 years of service
- 25 days after 5 years of service
- Use company portal to request time off

### Professional Development
- $2,000 annual learning budget per employee
- Conference attendance encouraged
- Internal training programs available

## Safety Protocols

### Office Safety
- Emergency exits clearly marked
- First aid kits available on each floor
- Fire drills conducted quarterly

### Data Security
- Use strong passwords (minimum 12 characters)
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Never share login credentials
- Report security incidents immediately

### Travel Safety
- Register all business travel in advance
- Use approved transportation providers
- Maintain emergency contact information
