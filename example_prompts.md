## Tips for Better Results

### Be Specific
Instead of: "Tell me about policies"
Try: "What is the company's remote work policy for full-time employees?"

### Use Context
Instead of: "How do I submit?"
Try: "How do I submit an expense report for travel expenses?"

### Ask Follow-up Questions
- "Can you provide more details about [specific topic]?"
- "What are the exceptions to this policy?"
- "Who should I contact if I have questions about this?"

### Category-Specific Searches
Use the category filter to search within specific document types:
- HR documents for employee-related questions
- Policies for company guidelines
- SOPs for operational procedures
- Technical for IT and system-related queries
